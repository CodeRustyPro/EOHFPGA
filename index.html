The Problem

Why Monte Carlo?

Financial option pricing requires simulating thousands of possible market paths. Traditional analytical solutions cannot handle complex, path-dependent derivatives like Asian or barrier options. Monte Carlo methods solve this by simulating random price walks, but running ten thousand paths on a CPU takes seconds, not the milliseconds that live trading demands. Hardware acceleration bridges this gap.

Path-Dependent Pricing

Asian, barrier, and lookback options depend on the entire price path, not just the endpoint.

Accuracy at Scale

More simulation paths means better accuracy. 10,000+ paths with hardware acceleration. No latency penalty.

Real-Time Speed

Trading desks need pricing in milliseconds. FPGA acceleration delivers deterministic, sub-second latency.

Our Approach
